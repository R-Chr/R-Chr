I build structural models of disordered materials and the computational infrastructure needed to validate them. My work sits at the intersection of **machine learning interatomic potentials (MLIPs)**, **ab initio molecular dynamics**, and **experimental scattering observables** (neutron/X-ray diffraction, S(Q), G(r)).
 
Currently at **Tohoku University**, previously at **Aalborg University (AAU)**.
 
---
 
### Research focus
 
**ML potentials for disordered matter** :  **High-throughput glass workflows** :  **Structural characterisation**

### Open source
 
#### [vitrum](https://github.com/R-Chr/vitrum) &nbsp;![Python](https://img.shields.io/badge/-Python-3776ab?style=flat-square&logo=python&logoColor=white)
 
Analysis and workflow package for simulated glass structures.
- Partial and total structure factors S(Q) and G(r) for neutron and X-ray scattering
- Ring analysis, coordination statistics, bond angle distributions
> **WIP** — APIs and functionality are subject to change. Contributions and bug reports welcome.
